Shakshuka Recipe

Description

This recipe is absolutely incredible! The flavors from the dish are unique and vibrant. Highly recommend this dish to anyone!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Â¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • (1) 28-ounce can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 small bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 small bunch fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Toss in onions and bell pepper
  2. Add garlic
  3. Add spices
  4. Add tomatoes
  5. Add eggs
  6. Let it simmer
  7. Top with cilantro and parsley
  8. Ready to serve.
